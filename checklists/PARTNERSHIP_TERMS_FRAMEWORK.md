#### **Phase 2 - Growth Orders (Months 4-12):**
- **Minimum Order**: ¬£1,000 per strain

- **Total Monthly**: ¬£8,000-20,000 growth phase

#### **Phase 3 - Scale Orders (Year 2+):**
- **Minimum Order**: ¬£2,000 per strain
